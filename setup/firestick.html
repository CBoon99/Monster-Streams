Amazon Firestick

Use the player package you were given (for example Monster TiviMate or Monster Smarters). Keep the device on power and Wi-Fi with a clear line of sight to your router when possible.

  1. Install the APK or app build from your onboarding link.
  2. Enable unknown sources only for the installer you trust (e.g. Downloader), then disable when finished.
  3. Open the app, add your playlist or portal credentials exactly as supplied.
  4. Open Live TV, Movies, and Series once so the guide can populate.
  5. Before big events, restart the stick and close background apps.
