Smart TV

Many Smart TVs cannot sideload arbitrary APKs. Use only the player or workflow Monster Streams confirms for your TV OS (Android TV, Fire TV built-in, Samsung/LG where applicable).

  • Update the television firmware and the streaming app to the latest stable release.
  • Use Ethernet or place the TV closer to your mesh node; 4K live sports is sensitive to packet loss.
  • Disable energy-saving picture modes that insert frame interpolation if you notice judder on fast sports.
  • If the onboard app store lacks your player, a Firestick or Android TV box is often the simplest path—see Firestick or Android.
