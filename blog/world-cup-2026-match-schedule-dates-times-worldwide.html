Kickoff Times: US (ET), UK & Thailand

Host venues use local stadium time. For viewers abroad, convert from Eastern Time (ET) during the North American summer (EDT, UTC−4). Approximate offsets:

  • UK (BST): typically +5 hours vs ET (e.g. 15:00 ET → 20:00 UK)
  • Thailand / Phuket (ICT): typically +11 hours vs ET — often early morning the next calendar day (e.g. 15:00 ET Tue → 02:00 Wed ICT)
  • US West Coast (PT): typically −3 hours vs ET
